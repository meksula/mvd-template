About Smart Explorer v 1.0

Smart Explorer v 1.0 allows you to easily explore the area where you are.
You can quickly locate the nearest historical place that you can visit.

RESTful API collects data about historical places, ratings, reviews, statistics of visits, etc. New features that will allow you to plan routes will be added soon. You can also become a content creator. All you have to do is register and the objects you create can be searched by visitors.
